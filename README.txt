================================================================================
                   METHOD 1: PASTE ITINERARY (EASIEST!)
================================================================================

This method takes your messy trip notes and turns them into a visual itinerary
in 3 simple steps:

STEP 1: Gather Your Trip Information
────────────────────────────────────────
Collect everything about your trip in ANY format:
- Email confirmations (hotels, flights, tours)
- Booking references and confirmation numbers
- Dates, times, addresses
- Notes from travel agents
- Spreadsheets or documents
- Even messy handwritten notes!

Don't worry about organizing it - just have it ready!

STEP 2: Use AI to Structure Your Trip
────────────────────────────────────────
1. Open the file: AI_TEXT_CONVERSION_PROMPT.txt
2. Copy the ENTIRE prompt
3. Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI assistant
4. Paste YOUR messy trip details at the bottom
5. AI will convert it to structured text format

The AI prompt is specifically designed to convert your chaos into a clean,
structured format that Trip Visualizer can read.

STEP 3: Paste Into Trip Visualizer
────────────────────────────────────────
1. Open Trip Visualizer app in your browser
2. Select "Paste Itinerary" in the sidebar
3. Paste the structured text from AI
4. Click "🚀 Generate Itinerary"
5. Watch your visual itinerary appear!

That's it! No JSON, no technical formatting required.

================================================================================
                         TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

❌ ERROR: "Could not parse itinerary"
✓ SOLUTION: Make sure you used the AI_TEXT_CONVERSION_PROMPT.txt file
✓ Copy the COMPLETE prompt (don't modify it)
✓ Paste ALL your trip details to AI
✓ Copy the COMPLETE output from AI

❌ ERROR: Map doesn't show location
✓ SOLUTION: The AI should use standard location names (Phuket, Krabi, Bangkok, etc.)
✓ Check the structured text has location after the dash: "DAY 1 - Dec 22, 2025 - Phuket"

❌ ERROR: Booking doesn't appear
✓ SOLUTION: Each booking should be on its own line
✓ Format: Time | Type | Name | Location | Ref | Status
✓ Use pipe | character to separate fields

❌ ERROR: Times are wrong
✓ SOLUTION: Use 12-hour format (9:30 AM, 2:00 PM)
✓ For time ranges: "9:30 AM - 4:00 PM"

================================================================================
                         TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
================================================================================

✓ Include as much detail as possible in your original notes
✓ Mention booking platforms (Booking.com, Tripadvisor, Agoda, etc.)
✓ Include confirmation numbers and references
✓ Specify exact times with AM/PM
✓ Add full addresses for hotels and meeting points
✓ Note special instructions or requirements
✓ Set correct status (confirmed/pending/optional/cancelled)

The more detail you give the AI, the better structured output you'll get!
