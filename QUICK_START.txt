================================================================================

DAY 1 - Dec 22, 2025 - Phuket
2:00 PM | Hotel | Grand Hotel | Address | Booking.com #REF | Confirmed
Notes: Check-in instructions

DAY 2 - Dec 23, 2025 - Phuket
9:30 AM - 4:00 PM | Tour | Snorkeling | Marina | Tripadvisor #REF | Confirmed

================================================================================
                          FORMAT QUICK REFERENCE
================================================================================

BOOKING LINE FORMAT:
   [Time] | [Type] | [Name] | [Location] | [Platform #Ref] | [Status]

SUPPORTED TYPES:
   Hotel, Flight, Tour, Ferry, Dining, Spa

SUPPORTED LOCATIONS:
   Phuket, Krabi, Koh Samui, Bangkok, Phi Phi Islands, Koh Phangan, Chiang Mai

SUPPORTED STATUS:
   Confirmed, Pending, Optional, Cancelled

TIME EXAMPLES:
   2:00 PM
   9:30 AM - 4:00 PM

SEPARATOR:
   Use pipe | character between fields
